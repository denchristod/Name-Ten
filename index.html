Name Ten!

The Party Quiz Game

How to Play

  • 1
    Two teams take turns answering questions across multiple rounds.
  • 2
    Each question asks you to name 10 things in a category (e.g. "Name 10 things in a fridge").
  • 3
    The reading team reads the question aloud. The playing team has 90 seconds to call out answers.
  • 4
    The team holding the phone taps each item when it's named correctly.
  • 5
    Items are worth 1–4 points based on difficulty. Harder answers = more points!
  • 6
    After time's up, there's a correction window to fix any mistakes before passing the phone.
  • 7
    After all rounds, the team with the most points wins!
1pt — Easy
